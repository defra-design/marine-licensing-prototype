Latitude and longitude can be written in different formats, including:

  • decimal degrees (DD) - 41.40338, 2.17403
  • degrees, minutes and seconds (DMS) - 41°24'12.2"N 2°10'26.5"E
  • degrees and decimal minutes (DMM) - 41 24.2028, 2 10.4418

Only use the decimal degree (DD) format to enter your coordinates.

Latitude ranges from -90 to 90, and longitude ranges from -180 to 180. For example, London has a latitude of 51.5074 and a longitude of -0.1278. They are often written together, separated by a comma. Latitude will always be the first number, followed by longitude. You need to enter these 2 numbers in separate fields.
