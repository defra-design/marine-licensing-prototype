MPAs are an important part of the marine environment. Any activity within 200 metres of an MPA may need a marine licence.

MPAs include:

  • Highly Protected Marine Areas (HPMA)
  • Marine Conservation Zones (MCZs)
  • Special Areas of Conservation (SACs)
  • Special Protection Areas (SPAs)
  • Ramsar sites - a wetland designated as being of international importance
  • Sites of Special Scientific Interest (SSSIs)

You can check whether the activity falls within 200 metres of an MPA using the GIS tool.
