Check if you need a marine licence

Use this tool to find out if your activity:

  • falls within the jurisdiction of the Marine Management Organisation (MMO)
  • is exempt from marine licensing
  • is suitable for self-service marine licensing
  • requires a standard marine licence

Where applicable, you will be directed to the appropriate service.

Find out more about the MMO's jurisdiction, exemptions and self-service marine licensing.

Before you start

Ensure you read MMO's marine licence guidance. You will need to know:

  • where your activity will take place
  • what your activity is
  • what it will involve

The guidance provides a basic understanding of the factors that determine if a marine licence is needed. It also explains the differences between the various services available.
