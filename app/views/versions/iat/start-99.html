Check if you need a marine licence - Article 99

Use this tool to find out:

  • If your activity falls within the jurisdiction of the MMO
  • If an exemption may apply (where appropriate)
  • If your activity is suitable for self-service marine licensing (where appropriate)

Where applicable, you will be directed to the appropriate service.

Find out more about the MMO's jurisdiction, exemptions and self-service marine licensing.

Before you start

Ensure you read MMO's marine licence guidance. You will need to know:

  • What your activity is
  • Where your activity will take place
  • What it will involve

The guidance will provide a basic understanding of the factors that help determine whether a marine licence is required and explain the difference between various services available.
