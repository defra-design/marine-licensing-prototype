Creating a Defra account as an agent or third party working on behalf of someone else

If you're acting as an intermediary you cannot create a Defra account on behalf of your client to notify us of an exemption.

You need to get the organisation you're representing to create the account, if they do not already have one, and invite you to it as an intermediary.

To create an account, first they need to sign in to, or create, a GOV.UK One Login or Government Gateway account. Then select Get Permission for Marine Work (opens in new tab) from the list and create a Defra account.
