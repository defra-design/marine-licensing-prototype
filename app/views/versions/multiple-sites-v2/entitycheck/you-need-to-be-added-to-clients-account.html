You need to be added to your client's account

You cannot submit a notification until your client has set you up.

What needs to happen

You must ask your client to:

  1. Register for a Defra account (if they do not already have one).
  2. Go to 'Manage account users'.
  3. Invite you as an intermediary.
  4. Give you access to the 'Get permission for marine work' service.

Once you have received the invitation email and accepted it, you can return to this service.
