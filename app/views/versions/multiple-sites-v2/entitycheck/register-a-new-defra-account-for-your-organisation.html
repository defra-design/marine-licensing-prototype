Register a new Defra account for your organisation

You must create an account specifically for the business or organisation.

What you will need

To register an organisation, you will need:

  • the Companies House number (if the business is limited)
  • the main contact details for your business
  • a Government Gateway user ID or GOV.UK One Login

If you do not have a Government Gateway ID or One Login, you can create one in the next step.
