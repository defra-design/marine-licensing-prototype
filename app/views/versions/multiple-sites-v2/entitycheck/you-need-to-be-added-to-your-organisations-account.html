You need to be added to your organisation's Defra account

You cannot submit an exemption until your organisation has set you up.

What needs to happen

You must ask an administrator of your organisation's Defra account to:

  1. Go to 'Manage account users'.
  2. Invite you as a team member.
  3. Give you access to the 'Get permission for marine work' service.

Once you have received the invitation email and accepted it, you can return to this service.

If you do not know who your organisation's administrator is, contact Defra account support:

  • Customer.Identity-Support@defra.gov.uk
  • 03459 33 55 77 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm)
