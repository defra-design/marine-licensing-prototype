Exempt activity notification for an organisation

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "If you do not set up your Defra account correctly your exempt activity notification will not be valid. This is because it will not be registered to the organisation the exemption is for.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

If you're already registered with the organisation the exemption is for, go back and select the correct organisation.

You're an employee of the organisation

If the organisation does not have a Defra account

{{ govukInsetText({ html: "If your organisation is a local authority or other government department, you must contact marine.consents@marinemanagement.org.uk to create a new Defra account." }) }}

To create a new Defra account for your organisation, you need to:

  1. Go to Your Defra account.
  2. Select Register a new account in the Your accounts section.
  3. Select a business or organisation account.
  4. Continue the Defra account creation process.

Get invited to an existing Defra account

Ask your organisation's Defra account administrator to invite you to your organisation's Defra account.

Contact marine.consents@marinemanagement.org.uk if you do not know who the administrator is.

You're the agent or intermediary for a client organisation

If your client already has a Defra account

You need to ask your client to invite you to their Defra account as an intermediary.

If your client does not have a Defra account

Ask your client to create a Defra account and invite you to it as an intermediary.

To create an account, your client needs to take the following steps:

  • Sign in to or create a Defra account for their business or organisation.
  • They'll create a Defra account using GOV.UK Login or Government Gateway.
  • Invite you as an intermediary under Manage account users.
  • Give you access to the Get permission for marine work service.
