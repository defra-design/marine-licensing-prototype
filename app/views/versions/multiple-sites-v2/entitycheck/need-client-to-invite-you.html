You need to get your client to invite you to their Defra account

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "If you do not set up your Defra account correctly your exemption notification will not be valid. This is because it will not be registered to the organisation the exemption is for.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

You cannot create a Defra account as an agent or intermediary working on behalf of someone else.

You need to get the organisation you're representing to create the account, if they do not already have one, and invite you to it as an intermediary.

To create an account, first they need to sign in to, or create, a GOV.UK One Login or Government Gateway account. Then select Get Permission for Marine Work from the list and create a Defra account.
