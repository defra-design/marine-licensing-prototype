Based on your answers, your activity is exempt under Article 17a of the Marine Licensing (Exempted Activities) Order 2011 (opens in new tab).

Your activity is only exempt on the condition that you provide further information before starting.

This is a 'beta' service - find out what this means for you (opens in new tab).

To use the service, you'll need:

  • details of the site where the activity will take place
  • information about what the activity involves
  • start and end dates for your activity

How to access this service:

  1. Sign in to, or create, a GOV.UK One Login or Government Gateway account. You'll use your One Login or Government Gateway account to sign into this service each time you visit.

    If you're accessing this service as an organisation do not use your personal One Login or Government Gateway account.

  2. You then need to create a Defra account, if you do not already have one, to send us your information. You can use your business or organisation details. Find out how to create a Defra account (opens in new tab).
