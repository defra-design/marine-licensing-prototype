Pre-application consultation

Pre-application consultation is informal. It means speaking to public groups or organisations before submitting your application.

We ask because it helps us understand the context of your application and prepare for the formal public consultation.

Public consultation

After we assess your application, we will open a formal public consultation. This is a legal requirement.

The formal consultation:
