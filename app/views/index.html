The scenario for this iteration

You're a university student looking to carry out some research involving analysis of sediments from just off the coast of Worthing, by the pier.

You need to carry out a survey taking 1 sample within a 100m of the pier.

You'll travel to the sample sites by boat and lower a grab overboard. The grab will be used to extract 0.3m3 of material from the surface of the sea floor.

The material removed will be returned to the vessel and taken anyway for testing and analysis.

A fellow student advised that you might need to get permission from the Marine management organisation before you carry out this activity.
